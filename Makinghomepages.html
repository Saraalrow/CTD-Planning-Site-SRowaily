Postive and Negtive pointers about Creating a Webpage in FireWorks

Postives

  • It's stright foreward.
  • The html of the file is automatically placed by Fireworks.
  • The CSS is autimatically put into place easily without using code.
  • Fireworks has pages and states instead of just layers. This is great for creating multi-page designs and walking through the design w/o having to turn layers on and off to demo.

Negtives

  • It's not as complex as Dreamweaver, which restricts the user.
  • The html can't be controled by the user, or it doesn't show up on Fireworks
