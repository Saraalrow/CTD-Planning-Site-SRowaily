Free Dress Code

Non-Uniform (Free Dress) Days are typically organized monthly to raise funds for designated charities or events. The following guidelines are in place for non-uniform days:

  • Students are not allowed to wear very tight or revealing clothing, including mini-skirts, hot pants, crop tops, low-cut tops or clothing that is see-through.
  • Students are not allowed to wear any article of clothing that carries obscene language, pictures or symbols, or promotes alcohol, drugs or tobacco related products.
  • Students are not allowed to wear clothing that is torn, ripped or ragged.
